Privacy Policy for AgriAgent

Last Updated: June 1, 2026

AgriAgent ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application AgriAgent (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Profile picture (if you choose to upload)
  • Address and location data

User Role Specific Information

  • Farmers: Land details, crop information, labour requirements
  • Labourers: Skills, experience, availability, wage expectations
  • Contractors: Team size, equipment owned, service areas
  • Buyers: Purchase history, delivery address, order preferences

Device Information

  • Device model and operating system version
  • Unique device identifiers
  • App version and usage data

Usage Data

  • Features you use
  • Time spent in the app
  • Listings viewed or created
  • Transaction history
  • Ratings and reviews you submit

Location Data

  • GPS coordinates to help you find nearby labourers, equipment, and shops
  • Approximate location based on IP address

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Connect farmers with labourers, equipment owners, contractors, and buyers
  • Verify user identities to maintain trust and safety on the platform
  • Improve and personalize your app experience
  • Send important notifications about your listings, bookings, and transactions
  • Process payments and prevent fraudulent activity
  • Provide customer support and respond to your inquiries
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve our services
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share information only in the following circumstances:

With Other Users

  • Your profile information is visible to other users
  • Contact details may be shared to facilitate transactions (e.g., rental agreements)
  • Ratings and reviews you receive are publicly visible within the app

With Service Providers

  • Payment processors to handle transactions securely
  • Cloud hosting providers to store your data
  • Analytics providers to help us improve the app

Legal Requirements

  • To comply with applicable laws and regulations
  • To respond to lawful requests from public authorities
  • To protect our legal rights and prevent fraud

4. Data Security

  • All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS
  • Passwords are hashed and salted before storage
  • We conduct regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Access to personal data is restricted to authorized personnel only
  • We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information

5. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt-out: Disable marketing communications
  • Data Portability: Export your data in a machine-readable format
  • Location: Disable location services in your device settings
  • Camera/Storage: Deny or revoke permission in device settings

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@agriagent.com.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data as long as your account remains active. Upon account deletion:

  • Your data is permanently removed within 30 days
  • Transaction records may be retained longer to comply with tax and legal obligations
  • Anonymized analytics data may be kept indefinitely for service improvement

7. Children's Privacy

AgriAgent is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately and we will delete such information.

8. Location Services

We request location access to help you find nearby labourers, equipment, and shops. Location data is:

  • Collected only when you grant permission
  • Used only while you use relevant features
  • Not stored historically unless needed for a specific transaction

You can disable location services in your device settings at any time, but this may affect app functionality.

9. Camera and Storage Permissions

We request camera and storage access to allow you to:

  • Upload profile pictures
  • Add photos of equipment for rental
  • Upload produce images for marketplace listings
  • Share photos of problems or issues for community support

You can deny or revoke these permissions at any time. Core app features will still work without these permissions.

10. Third-Party Services

Our app may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending an in-app notification for significant changes

Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the modified policy.
