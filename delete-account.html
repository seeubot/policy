Delete Your AgriAgent Account

Method 1: In-App Deletion

  1. Open the AgriAgent app
  2. Tap the Settings icon in the top right corner
  3. Tap "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm deletion

Method 2: Email Request

Send an email with subject "Account Deletion Request" to the email address listed in the app Settings.

Include your registered email address and full name.

Requests are processed within 7 business days.

What Gets Deleted

  • Profile information (name, photo, phone)
  • Equipment and produce listings
  • Forum posts and solutions
  • Ad campaigns and payment history
